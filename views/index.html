PROJECTS

Sacred Tribe Energy Healing

Designed and developed personalised e-commerce site based on client's vision and halved their monthly website fees. Shopify was integrated to handle secure payments, product inventory and to provide the client with full control of the business.

Description and Role

Client had created a site with a drag-and-drop website provider that acheived a high volume of visitors but a low conversion rate. The site required a personal design and identity that matched the product but the limited available options made the site look outdated and as a result customers did not find it trustworthy. I designed and developed the site based on the clients ideas and integrated a Shopify Lite, giving their site an identity and a reduction in monthly subscription fees.

Difficulties

The client's original site lacked structure and a natural flow that would make navigation easier for users and lead to increased sales. The client also needed the freedom to add new products, update prices and edit product information. In addition to posting blogs and adding links to parter sites. Most importantly, the client was looking for a cheaper alternative to the service they were using as the subscription fees continued to rise and there was a product limit set for each site.

Solutions

The site was completely redesigned from scratch, creating a more cohesive and consistent feel to the site while maintaining the personal feel of the original site. The site was structured in a way to highlight the key points of the site and provide a flow that makes navigation easier for the users. By hosting the site on Heroku and integrating a Shopify Lite store in the site I was able to give the client full control of the site ... inventory etc. which led to monthly service fees being reduced to 1/3 of the original price

Notable Features

  • Secure payments with Shopify
  • Full control of product inventory, shipping, pricing and discounts
  • Personalised design
  • Responsive design
  • Product filtering
  • Material list with images
  • Secret log in for client to add/edit blogs and links to partner sites
  • Custom client email
  • Sessions to store items in cart and wishlist for returning customers
